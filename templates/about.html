Welcome to About Us page of TextUtils.com! You will know everything about us here.

Textutils provides you the solution for whatever text edit task you desire, taking your writing business to another level by trimming it to only the features that aid you in dealing with the text. Where older applications, such as the likes of WordPad or Notepad, may cut it if you want a simple cut and paste procedure, there is more that one can achieve with the contents using the Textutils system.

With this platform, you get powerful tools that help you do your work in a shorter time. You can, with a single click of the mouse, remove unwanted punctuation, convert your entire text to all caps, get rid of extra spaces and unwanted new lines. Character count is also accurate from TextUtils, so that you can keep track of your writing easily.

Whether you want to edit a document in preparation for submission or merely clean up your notes, TextUtils is here for you to get the task done as fast and painlessly as possible. Join us at TextUtils, to find how easy it is to refine your text with our straight and effective tools!
